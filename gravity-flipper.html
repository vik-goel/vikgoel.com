Gravity Flipper

“A challenging new arcade title.” –Droidgamers
Collect the dots. Avoid the blocks. Reverse gravity.
Sounds easy enough? Try reversing the gravity of two worlds at the same time.

Using Java and LibGDX, I developed Gravity Flipper for the Ludum Dare game jam competition in 48 hours. It was a lot of fun making and has been enjoyed by thousands of people around the world!
