DYC USA

I volunteered for DYC USA by developing Android apps for them. Working with a small team of volunteer developers, we created seven karaoke apps together which allow people to play follow along to popular prayers.

Ensuring that the audio and text highlighting stayed in sync, making the app work in many different languages, and sharing code between many different apps presented interesting challenges for us to solve.
