Water Water

Run away from the evil geese in Water Water! Shoot the geese in your way with water to get them to start chasing you, jump over obstacles, and make sure to avoid the poop raining down from above. See how far you can make it in this endless runner.

3 friends and me made Water Water together. Making a pixel art water simulator was really fun and I think it turned out well. Everyone that we showed the game to had a lot of fun playing it and loved spraying the geese.
